Rapid React

Rapid React, the 2022 game for FRC, is a fast-paced airport-themed game in which robots on each alliance must collect "cargo" (balls in the colour of the alliances) and deposite them into one of the "hubs" (goal areas), as well as complete a climb in the "Hanger" (an area with rungs of differing heights) in order to earn points. More information about the game can be found on Wikipedia.
